Information Sheet and Consent Form



Dear Participant,

You are invited to participate in a research study conducted by The AI Research Group from Eduvos. The purpose of this study is to examine the impact of AI tools on the academic performance of IT students.



Purpose of the Study

This study aims to understand how AI-powered tools, such as ChatGPT, influence students' learning experiences, academic achievements, and perceptions of AI technology. Your participation will provide valuable insights into the effectiveness and ethical considerations of AI tool usage in education.



Data Usage

All data collected will be used solely for research purposes. The data will be anonymized and securely stored to protect your privacy. Only the research team will have access to the raw data.



Right to Withdraw

Your participation is voluntary, and you have the right to withdraw from the study at any time without penalty. If you choose to withdraw, any data you have provided will be excluded from the study and will not be used in the analysis.



Informed Consent

By proceeding to the survey, you acknowledge that you have read and understood the information provided above and agree to participate in the study. If you have any questions or concerns, please contact Occxlnce Creative Studio.
